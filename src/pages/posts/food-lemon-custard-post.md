# About This Recipe

Fun fact--I found this recipe on the back of a bottle of lemon custard-scented lotion! But don't worry, there's no lotion in this. In fact, it's a super simple recipe, and it's my go-to for a delicious treat when I don't want to cook for too long and am craving sweets.

**Servings:** 2

**Calories / Serving:** 353.2

# Ingredients

*FOR THE CUSTARD*
1. White sugar, 1/2 Cup
2. Cornstarch, 1/4 Cup
3. Salt, 1/4 tsp
4. Egg Yolks, 2
5. Water, 3/4 Cup
6. Lemon Juice, 1/3 Cup
7. Butter, 2 TBSP

*FOR THE WHIPPED TOPPING*
1. Heavy Cream, 1/4 Cup
2. Lemon Juice, 1 TBSP
3. Lemon Zest, 1 lemon's worth

# Directions

*FOR THE CUSTARD*
1. Add the *sugar, cornstarch,* & *salt* to a small saucepan.
2. Beat the *eggs* & *water* together in a small bowl.
3. Put the *saucepan* over **medium heat**, and, whisking constantly, add the *egg mixture*.
4. Simmer until the mixture thickens. (It should look a little thicker than pudding)
5. Remove from heat, stir in *lemon juice* & *butter*.
6. Carefully spoon *custard* into two tall glasses and set in fridge to cool. (At least 20-30 minutes)

When the custard is the desired temperature, move on to the whipped topping:

*FOR THE WHIPPED CREAM TOPPING*
1. Add *heavy cream* & *lemon juice* to a small bowl or pint-quart sized jar.
2. Whip, either by hand or machine, until soft peaks form.
3. Carefully spoon *whipped cream* on top of custard, and sprinkle the *lemon zest* on top.

Enjoy!!
